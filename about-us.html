Our Story

Elite Timepieces was founded in 1990 by Alex Johnson, a passionate watch collector and enthusiast. He wanted to share his love of luxury watches with others and provide a platform for people to purchase high-quality timepieces from the world's finest brands

We offer a stunning collection of luxury watches from the world's finest brands. We pride ourselves on offering exceptional customer service and ensuring that each purchase is a seamless and enjoyable experience

Today, Elite Timepieces is a well-respected name in the watch industry, known for its exceptional selection of luxury watches and dedication to customer service
